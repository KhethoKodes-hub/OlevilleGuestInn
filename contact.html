Frequently Asked Questions

What is the check-in/check-out time?

Check-in time is from 2:00 PM, and check-out time is by 10:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability.

Do you offer airport transportation?

We can arrange airport transportation services for our guests upon request. Please contact us in advance to make arrangements.

Is parking available?

Yes, we offer secure on-site parking for all our guests at no additional charge.

Do you have wheelchair accessible rooms?

Yes, we have wheelchair accessible rooms available. Please specify your needs when making a reservation.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit cards, and bank transfers. A deposit may be required for certain reservations.
