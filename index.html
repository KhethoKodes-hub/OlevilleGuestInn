Welcome to Oleville

Oleville Guest Inn and Conferencing is a deluxe guest accommodation located in Kuruman in the Northern Cape, just 2.7 km from Kuruman Mall. Our guest house offers a great home away from home experience with 5 Standard en-suite rooms and 4 Executive en-suite rooms. Oleville is the perfect platform to discover the vibrant and dynamic Kuruman touch.
