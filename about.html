Our Story

Oleville Guest Inn and Conferencing is a deluxe guest accommodation nestled in the heart of Kuruman in the Northern Cape. Strategically located just 2.7 km from Kuruman Mall, we offer the perfect blend of convenience and tranquility.

Established with a vision to provide exceptional hospitality experiences, Oleville has become synonymous with comfort, elegance, and authentic Northern Cape hospitality. Our guest house offers a true home away from home experience with our carefully designed 5 Standard en-suite rooms and 4 Executive en-suite rooms.

At Oleville, we believe that every guest deserves personalized attention and exceptional service. Whether you're visiting Kuruman for business or leisure, we strive to make your stay memorable with our warm hospitality and attention to detail.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide exceptional accommodation and conferencing experiences that exceed our guests' expectations through personalized service, luxurious comfort, and authentic Northern Cape hospitality.
